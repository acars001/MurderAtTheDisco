Detective: Hello, Ross. I have reason to believe you were present the night of Maria's murder. Is this correct?

Ross: I was at the studio earlier I was just dropping off maria's bag.

Detective: I'm afraid we have evidence against that statement. This means you are now a suspect in the murder of Maria.

Ross: Oh, okay.

Detective: Can you tell me why you were at the studio that night.

Ross: I was just meeting up with Zach. He can explain why.

Detective: That's strange cause Zach said you had just ran into each other. Please refrain from lying, it will only make you look worse.

Ross: Okay. I just bumped into Zach. I was actually looking for Steph.

Detective: Why did you not mention this earlier?

Ross: I suppose it's because I thought I'd look guilty. I was planning on finally asking her out so went looking for her at the studio but then I... I heard shouting. I tried to find out where it was coming from but by the time I had found the source my sister was already dead. I didn't know what to do, so I left. I bumped into Zach on the way out and I told him to delete the footage, I had hoped it would erase any evidence of me being at the studio. I swear I didn't do anything. Oh, and I didn't see anything either.

Detective: Did you not think to go and save your sister? Or even comfort Steph?

Ross: Honestly, I wanted to but I panicked and fled. You have to believe me!

Detective: Alright (quickly scribbling something down into his journal). Thank you for your cooperation. You may go.

Ross: Are you sure? Do you believe me?

Detective: I am sure. Please leave whenever you are ready.
